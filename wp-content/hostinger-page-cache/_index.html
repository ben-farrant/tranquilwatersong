5 Star luxury villa with private pool and conservation view

** FREE WIFI and CABLE TV plus FREE local Calls to Canada and USA **

Our home is a luxurious 5 bedroom property situated in the popular and tranquil settings of Watersong Resort. The resort is well away from the hustle & bustle of Kissimmee but only a short distance from Disney, Universal and the other major attractions of the region.

There is plenty of room in the villa which sleeps 11. We have two separate living areas, two games areas and a beautiful 30ft pool with plenty of space all around the pool to sit out and relax.

Four of the 5 bedrooms have their own LCD TV, the main living area has a large Cable TV with video-on-demand and DVD player.  In the loft area there is also a large TV, DVD player and games console  – XBOX 360 and a few child-friendly games – feel free to bring your own handsets and games.  At the far end of the loft area we have a pool table, and in the converted garage we have Air-hockey and Table-Football – so plenty of choice for those ‘in-days’ or evening family competitions!

Our villa is located just over the road from the resort’s clubhouse which has its own communal pool, well equipped Gym, Volley Ball Court and Children’s play area.

Watersong is about 5 minutes from the I4 and just two junctions down from the Disney exit. It takes around 15 minutes to get from Watersong to the main entrance to Walt Disney World. For basic amenities there is a choice of restaurants and supermarkets just 5 minutes away from the Watersong resort.

Whether you choose to hit the theme parks, play on one of the many golf courses nearby or just chill at the resort, we are sure you will enjoy your stay at our Tranquil Watersong Villa.
